Work remotely. Belong locally.

A coworking space in Barreiro for remote workers who want a dedicated place to work — and a real connection to the town they live in.

Why Moinho Novo

Dedicated workspace

Your own permanent desk, ready when you are. Set it up once, keep it how you like it, and become part of the community.

Flexible access

Use the space daily or book by the hour through our partner. Drop in when you need a productive place to work.

Great location

20 minutes from Lisbon by ferry. A 15-minute walk from the terminal, next to parks and supermarkets.

Phone booths

All work calls happen in the booths so the main workspace stays quiet and focused for everyone.

Dog friendly

Our Chief Welcome Officer, Nala, is always around for cuddles. Your furry friend is welcome too.

Tea, coffee and microwave

Help yourself to tea and coffee, bring your lunch or grab a barista-made coffee while you work.
