Meet Beth, John & Nala 🐶

Moinho Novo begin with a simple idea: remote work shouldn’t mean working alone or feeling disconnected from the place you live. After moving to Barreiro and working remotely ourselves, we realised how hard it can be to fully integrate into a community while spending most of the day behind a screen. We created Moinho Novo to change that. Our goal is to build a welcoming coworking space where locals and newcomers can work productively while becoming part of the community around them. Alongside the workspace, we hope to organise Portuguese language classes, volunteering opportunities, and small gatherings that help people feel more connected to Barreiro. You’ll probably also meet our Chief Welcome Officer, Nala. She’s Portuguese, has three legs, and was rescued after being hit by a car as a puppy while living on the streets. These days she specialises in greeting people and supervising the workspace. We’re excited to build something meaningful here and help more people feel at home in Barreiro.
